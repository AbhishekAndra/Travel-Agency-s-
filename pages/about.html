About Stackly

We're a small team obsessed with the kind of travel that stays with you — curated flights, stays, and journeys to the world's most quietly extraordinary places.

How We Started

Our Story

Stackly began with a simple frustration: most travel sites sell inventory. We wanted to sell trips worth remembering.

Founded by a small group of former guides and travel writers, we started by handpicking a dozen properties we'd stayed in ourselves. Twelve years later, that same standard — would we send our own family here? — still decides what makes it onto Stackly.

We're still small on purpose. Every itinerary is built by someone who has actually been there, not assembled from a spreadsheet.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The standards that decide what does — and doesn't — make it onto Stackly.

Craft Over Scale

We'd rather curate 500 exceptional stays than list 50,000 mediocre ones.

Radical Transparency

The price you see is the price you pay — no hidden fees, ever.

Local First

Every guide, driver, and host is someone who actually lives there.

Every Trip Is Personal

No templates. Each itinerary is built around the traveler, not a package.
